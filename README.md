Steps:
1. On the phone you want the app on, open the link in the browser (Safari on iPhone, Chrome on Android). The page should load and look exactly like it does on your computer.
2. (iPhone) Tap the Share icon at the bottom of Safari (the square with an arrow pointing up). Scroll down in the menu that pops up and tap "Add to Home Screen." Confirm the name, tap Add. This only works in Safari — Chrome on iPhone can't do this step, it's an Apple restriction.
   (Android) In Chrome, you'll usually see a banner pop up near the bottom saying "Install app" or "Add to Home screen" — just tap it. If no banner appears, tap the three-dot menu in the top right, then look for "Install app" or "Add to Home screen" in the list.
3. You should now see an icon (the amber die icon) sitting right there with your other apps. Tapping it opens the roll console full-screen, no browser address bar or buttons around it — it behaves like a real installed app from that point on.
